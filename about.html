Who We Are

We are a trusted company dedicated to providing clients with specialized capabilities in sourcing, purchasing, and expediting to precisely meet their needs. Our expertise lies in the sourcing and supply of spares, tools, equipment, and machinery for the Oil and Gas sectors. Leveraging our international supply network, we can source and deliver any item globally to fulfill our client's requirements. In instances where the desired product is not in our catalog, we specialize in procuring bespoke products tailored to your specifications.


Reliability in your supplier is crucial for receiving genuine, high-quality products. At VENOMI INTEGRATED SERVICES LIMITED, we believe that better supplies lead to better energy.

Our collaborative approach involves working in teams, fostering communication and mutual support. Shared experiences strengthen our ability to enhance service quality for our clients, providing them with a single point of contact. Our team members are encouraged to build strong relationships with clients, recognizing the significance of meeting their needs for customer retention and new business acquisition.

We take pride in our business, backed by the extensive product knowledge and experience of our technical buyers. Three key reasons make us the preferred choice for our customers:

1. Our streamlined supply chain enables us to procure products at discounts not achievable by our customers independently.
2. Our commitment to service ensures faster delivery times compared to in-house channels.
3. Our broad technical knowledge allows us to quickly secure 'hard-to-find' products.

We have established connections with various Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs), enabling us to promptly respond to your inquiries. Our direct sourcing and expediting processes involve our geographically located personnel in the US, UK, Canada, and Europe, where materials and equipment are manufactured.
