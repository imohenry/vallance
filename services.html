Overview

Venomi Integrated Services Limited specializes in tailored sourcing and procurement services, concentrating on acquiring software, equipment, machinery, and chemicals for companies in the oil and gas sector. With our strong European presence and direct connections to over 700 manufacturers and suppliers, we have the capability to source and procure a wide range of products for our clients, ensuring all their requirements are met.

Our primary objective is to drive down our clients' procurement costs by negotiating the best prices in all transactions we conduct on their behalf. We actively follow up on deliveries to ensure that materials are supplied according to the specified requirements.

Oilfield Chemicals

We offer a comprehensive range of production chemicals, additives for drilling/cementing/workover, and treatments for refinery applications. Our offerings include drilling fluid products, treating chemicals, and site-specific products and programs for clients.

Calibration

We provide accredited calibration across various disciplines in electrical, dimensional, and physical parameters. Serving as a full-service provider, we are the single point of contact for all instrument calibration, service, and administration needs.

Calibration is crucial for meeting ISO900, obtaining traceability, adhering to official regulations, optimizing quality, saving energy, achieving optimum machine performance, minimizing reprocessing costs, reducing waste of raw materials, and mitigating pollution.

About Venomi

We are a trusted company dedicated to providing clients with specialized capabilities in sourcing, purchasing, and expediting to precisely meet their needs. Our expertise lies in the sourcing and supply of spares, tools, equipment, and machinery for the Oil and Gas sectors. Leveraging our international supply network, we can source and deliver any item globally to fulfill our client's requirements. In instances where the desired product is not in our catalog, we specialize in procuring bespoke products tailored to your specifications.
